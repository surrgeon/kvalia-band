The album recording was initiated beneath a flooded bridge in the spring of 2019 in the village of Minino, then instruments were re-recorded multiple times in Krasnoyarsk during the autumn of the same year. Finally, the album was mixed in spring 2020 in Tbilisi

Drum Machine, Sampler, Voice — Aleksander Maznichenko
